There is no place anyone would rather be on a beautiful summer day than the backyard deck, in a comfortable chair, sipping a cool drink. And yet, this idyllic scenario can so easily be spoiled if the sun is too hot or the rain is coming down. This is why Terry and Oksanna Zwarych of Regina fell in love with the almost magical qualities of these covers and started Posh Patio Deck Covers Inc. Saskatchewan’s exclusive dealers for Natural Light Patio covers. Helping to extend your outdoor entertaining space and outdoor entertaining season. Start enjoying your deck earlier and keep going later in the year. That is why they want to help you create your own outdoor Oasis.

We meet many of our customers by word-of-mouth advertising and we host Open Houses throughout the summer months. This is a great opportunity for potential clients to see an actual patio cover in a local backyard and to discuss both what we do and how we do it with a current owner. We invite you to keep an eye out for our signs and newspaper ads!

In 2004 we received numerous requests to modify our greenhouse structures to facilitate backyard needs, and we’ve been actively selling our patio covers ever since. In 2010 a new management team further expanded our explosive patio cover business to include most states and provinces. Our corporate successes speak to both the demand for the products and services we provide, and to the manner and integrity in which we bring them to the consumer. Much to be thankful for!

Our many satisfied clients will attest that covers designed, produced, and installed by Natural Light Patio Covers far exceed their initial expectations. The sales staff is knowledgeable and informative, the factory-trained installation teams are courteous and professional, and the products used are of state-of-the-art quality, guaranteed to give you years of maintenance-free enjoyment!
