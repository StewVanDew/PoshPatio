Create your own outdoor oasis!

Posh Patio Deck Covers is Saskatchewan’s exclusive dealer for Natural Light Patio Covers.

Natural Light Patio Covers bring the sun and shade together by blocking the sun’s harmful rays and protecting your family from the elements. Now you can expand your outdoor space without diminishing the wonderful, life-enhancing effects of natural sunlight! The ACRYLITE® sheeting creates a soft and radiant sunlit glow while reflecting heat and blocking 100% of the harmful UV rays. Even rooms adjacent to your Natural Light Patio Cover are brighter and more enjoyable! With a wide variety of applications and sheeting options your Natural Light Patio Covers can be fully customized to suit a broad variety of exterior applications, including patio and deck covers, pool and hot tub enclosures, carports, sunrooms and even hobby greenhouses. We offer exceptional services and offer a comprehensive 30-year warranty to back up our ACRYLITE® products and installation. Natural Light Patio Covers are a great environmental choice:

  • All materials used for our products are 100% recyclable.
  • Our structures are built to last, so they will not end up in the landfill.
  • Our warehouse is fully powered by our own wind turbine for a reduced carbon footprint.
